Title:

Plant Lamp

Issue:

The design presented in the Fab Lab is the proposal of an ecological lamp that generates electricity and oxygen at the same time. This will help save energy and help the environment.It will be designed on top of a common plant, the only thing that is needed is the sand, the focus will be a diamond to give it a better aesthetic but it can also be of any shape..

2.-The proposal is of high social, economic impact.

Hypothesis:

• The proposal on ecological lamps is of high social impact since it involves the development of technology and the use of clean energies to provide light to rural areas that hardly have access to the electricity grid and that even resort to the use of candles or candlesticks with fuel, which report many cases of fatal accidents. • The proposal on ecological lamps is of high economic impact, because it is an idea of ​​sustainability with the environment and that can contribute with lighting or as an energy source to charge an electronic device. If a study of production and sales costs is carried out, a profit can be obtained from a positive margin, for which reason it is advisable to study in depth the market of this niche, as well as experiences of success from abroad that allow to scale up the production in a stage more advanced a posterior.
