DEFINE

According to our Benchmarking of the ecological lamp with the sales pages that have the same product system in an alternative source of energy can be taken as criteria to evaluate the following expressed in the table. We also take the four trends that must be taken into account due to the risks and opportunities involved:

 

DEFINE

1. Technological advances and the speed of creation. When using an alternative energy helping the environment.

2. Globalization and the loss of geographic advantages. We focus on the fact that this product is not a market established in our country only foreign pages for distribution that vary their price

3. The growing power of consumers and their ability to obtain what they want, when they want and how they want it. This will be reported through a page giving the details of the product from its preparation to its completion for our customers to be informed.

4. The amount of information we have available about our clients today

Nowadays technology allows a competitor of the same product and a consumer to buy it anywhere in the world. Currently, new competitors also appear that do not always come from the same industry and offer solutions for customers more quickly. But we focus on our country since this new technology does not have a high impact.
